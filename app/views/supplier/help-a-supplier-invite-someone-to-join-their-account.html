Help a supplier invite someone to join their account

This design helps a supplier to invite a team member to access their account.

How the design works

Use this design if you need to:

  • sign users up to an existing account
  • help teams collaborate

From their account page, suppliers can invite team members to join the account.

Invited team members are emailed a link to sign in to the account. The email says who invited them.

Once signed in, team members can see everything in the account. They can also, if they want, invite other team members.

Any team members that no longer need access to the account can be removed.

A confirmation banner appears at the top of the page to say the team member has been removed.

Why this is useful

Suppliers have told us they don’t always work alone. They need other team members to do different tasks or provide sign-off.

This design helps them to collaborate with their teams.

They also need to be able to remove people from the account who have left the organisation.

Things to consider

To improve this design, it might be worth thinking about:

  • having a main administrator (‘super user’) who has control over all access rights
  • if you had a main administrator, you’d need the ability to change that role
  • the wording we use, for instance ‘contributors’ could be a bit unclear
  • allowing user to remove themselves from the account
