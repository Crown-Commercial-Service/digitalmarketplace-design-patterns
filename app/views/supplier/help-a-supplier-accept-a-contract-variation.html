Help a supplier accept a contract variation

This design helps suppliers agree to a change in their original framework agreement and call-off contract.

How the design works

Use this design if you need:

  • to show users what‘s changed
  • users to be able to accept the changes

To accept changes to the framework agreement’s terms and conditions, suppliers need to:

  • sign in to their accounts
  • read the contract variation and see what’s changed
  • click the accept button to agree the changes

Components used in the design

How we show suppliers the text changes has been discussed in the GOV.UK design system.

Why this is useful

On all other Crown Commercial Service (CCS) frameworks, contract variations need to be emailed to suppliers, printed out and signed, scanned and emailed back to CCS.

This is a much longer and more manual process. Avoiding this saves supplier time and effort.

Things to consider

This design had to be developed really quickly. That meant there was no time to test it with suppliers.

To improve the design, it would be worth:

  • accessibility testing the design, for example the green and red used may not provide adequate colour contrast
  • making the link to the contract variation more prominent in supplier accounts
