Help a supplier apply for work

This design helps suppliers apply for contracts on the Digital Outcomes and Specialists framework.

How the design works

Suppliers can search for contracts (or ‘opportunities’) on the Digital Marketplace without signing in. There’s also a link in their accounts to the advertised contracts.

To find the most relevant contract, suppliers need to use the search filters. The filters include:

  • location
  • category
  • whether applications are open or closed

To apply for a contract, suppliers have to answer a series of questions. This section of the design uses the checkbox and textarea component.

Suppliers confirm they have the skills the buyer asked for and then provide evidence of those skills. There’s a word limit on the evidence boxes.

Before submitting their application, suppliers are invited to check and edit their answers. This uses the check answers pattern.

When suppliers click the submit button a banner at the top of the page confirms:

  • their application has been submitted
  • what to do next

Why this is useful

This design guides suppliers through the process of finding and applying for relevant contracts.

By guiding suppliers through the questions it ensures they provide all the information the buyer needs to make a decision about their application.

Things to consider

To improve the design, you could consider:

  • making it clear that even if you’ve submitted your application, you can edit it up to the application deadline
  • emailing suppliers to confirm their application has been submitted and what happens next
