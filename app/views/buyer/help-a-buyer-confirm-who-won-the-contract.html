Help a buyer confirm who won the contract

This design helps buyers say which supplier they’ve awarded their contract to.

How the design works

This includes various elements from the design system, including:

  • the task list pattern
  • question pages
  • checkboxes

Buyers are prompted by email, or shown a link in their account, to say if the contract has been awarded.

If it has been awarded, buyers are asked to provide information including the:

  • name of the winning supplier
  • value of the contract
  • start and end date

Details can be published on the site, for example in a banner at the top of the requirements.

Why this is useful

This helps:

  • buyers see which suppliers are winning contracts
  • give buyers an opportunity to be open about their spending
  • suppliers who want to know who won the contract

Things to consider

Don’t use this if you don’t need to gather information from your user about their activities.

How to improve this

Buyers have to publish information about awarded contracts on Contracts Finder.

Ideally, we would pass the information about awarded contract to Contracts Finder directly for buyers.
