Help a buyer understand changes to a framework that might affect what they do next

This design lets us tell users information that might affect what they need to do next.

How the design works

We use blue banners above the page title to provide relevant information about the service.

This includes telling buyers that a:

  • framework will expire soon
  • framework has expired
  • new framework iteration has opened
  • service has been withdrawn

We also use the banners to reveal who won a contract.

Why this is useful

This design allows us to tell buyers about something:

  • that’s happening soon that could change their behaviour, for example a framework’s expiring
  • that could affect what they do next, for example a service has been withdrawn
  • that’s useful to know, for example a new framework iteration has opened

This information is placed at relevant points of the user journey, for example when buyers are about to download a list of services from a framework that’s about to expire.

Things to consider

Don’t use this design:

  • for errors
  • to confirm that an action has been completed
  • in body content
