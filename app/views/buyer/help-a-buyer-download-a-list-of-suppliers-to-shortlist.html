Help a buyer download a list of suppliers to shortlist

This design gives buyers a list of all the suppliers whose services match their requirements and are ready to be shortlisted.

How the design works

Once buyers have the list of all the eligible suppliers they can:

  • download a spreadsheet of eligible suppliers in an open document format (ods) or machine readable format (csv)
  • read guidance about what to do next, for example information about how to shortlist and evaluate suppliers
  • shortlist and evaluate suppliers

Why this is useful

It gives the buyers a list of everybody that’s applied and how those suppliers meet their requirements.

Things to consider

Don’t use this if there’s no need to download a list of eligible suppliers.

What we learnt

When we tested and implemented this design we learnt that:

  • most buyers downloaded their shortlist once they had reduced the list to less than 20 services
  • some buyers expected to see a work history or company profile at this point to help with shortlisting
  • having a shared access to a buyer account would encourage better team collaboration

How to improve this

This design could be improved if the shortlisting and evaluation process was all done online.
